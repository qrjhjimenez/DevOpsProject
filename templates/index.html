Current situation/scenario/practice

According to PSA, The production of chicken meat from 2020 has decreased 7.8 percent from the total production of 2019. From April to June of 2021, the total production was lower by 1.6 percent than the previous year of the same quarter.
Candling lamp is being used to check the appearance of the egg's embryo. The fertile egg will be used to produce another batch of chickens while the infertile egg will be sold on the market depending on its status.

Existing or similar solutions in the market

Egg Fertility Tester (Egg Candler)

Egg candling is an essential process during hatching. This tester was used to check if the embryos are developing correctly. Simply, by placing the egg on the egg tray tester where they will check the appearance of the eggs' embryo every 8th-10th day of the hatching.

Proposed Solution

The proposed solution is a system that can determine the fertility age and status of the egg. The machine will show the fertility status and recommended incubation environment based on the age of the egg on the screen LCD.

Objectives

  • To design a system that can give the accurate fertility test by removing the subjective analysis of the test workers.

  • To design a system that will be able to prevent egg exploder contamination on the incubation process of the eggs by identifying the fertility status as soon as possible.

  • To design a system that can provide an accurate sorting category of the egg based on the fertility status and age.
